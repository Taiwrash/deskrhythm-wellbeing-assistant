## The Starter Prompt

Build a “DeskRhythm”

Create a small personal app for people who sit in front for long hours that helps them listen to their body, not just the clock and privacy first

The tool should:

Gently track sitting time and screen intensity

Recommend when to stand, stretch, walk, or switch posture

Use smart intervals (not annoying timers)

Offer tiny, doable actions: stand for 2 minutes, walk while thinking, reset your posture, rest your eyes

End the day with a short health reflection: How did your body feel today?

No guilt.
No productivity pressure.
Just small nudges toward a healthier workday.

It can live as a desktop app, browser extension, menu bar tool, chatbot, or ambient assistant.

Build something that helps people finish work feeling better than when they started.

I love Golang for backend and react for frontend with accurate db and chances to have ai integration
